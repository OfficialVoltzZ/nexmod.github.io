NexMod University Admission Requirements

To ensure that our students have the best possible learning experience and contribute positively to our community, we have established the following admission requirements:


  • Age Requirement: Applicants must be at least 15 years old.

  • Discord Familiarity: Applicants should have a basic understanding of Discord and its features.

  • Written Application: All prospective students are required to submit a written application. The application should include:

    • A brief overview of your background and interests.

    An explanation of why you want to join NexMod University.

    Your goals and expectations from the academy.


  • Interview or Assessment: Shortlisted applicants will participate in a brief interview or assessment to evaluate their communication skills and motivation.

  • Basic Knowledge Test: Applicants will be required to complete a basic knowledge test covering fundamental concepts related to moderation, community management, and Discord.

  • Diversity and Inclusion Statement: As part of the application, we encourage you to submit a statement on your commitment to fostering diverse and inclusive online communities.

    • Note: We do not require a payment for admission and no portfolio is needed.

    Our goal is to create a supportive learning environment where students are engaged, motivated, and dedicated to honing their moderation skills. By adhering to these requirements, we ensure that every participant is prepared for the challenges and opportunities our academy offers.
